Summary of Water Talk

While Daisy was brushing her teeth in the bathroom, she didn’t turn off the tap and water was pouring into the washbasin and vanishing down the drain. Suddenly, she heard a sound that asked Daisy to turn off the tap. Daisy didn’t know who was talking, so her voice was faint and obeyed the voice. She then knew the drop of water was talking. It told Daisy its trip to the bathroom. A few days ago, it was floating comfortably in a cloud in Jiangxi, enjoying the view, then the cloud dropped it into a stream and it sped down the mountain into the river. Then it came into the water treatment works and the worker there gave it a thorough cleaning and added a few chemicals to it. Then it travelled in the pipes under the streets, it waited there until Daisy called it. After Daisy was finished with it, it would go into a sewage plant, then the workers there would pump it into the river and it would be back in the sea again. The sea was where it came from in the first place. Before the water drop left, it told Daisy that it’s precious, like liquid gold. Daisy asked what did it mean by liquid gold, but the water drop had already gone.
